Name of the business: Clean Touch

What they do: 
The Clean Touch is a deep cleaning service that covers areas of your personal space that cannot typically be addressed in regular cleaning. A deep cleaning service removes dirt and grime that accumulates over time and cannot be removed in a rush. A deep clean involves scrubbing and sanitizing every area of your house or car, from top to bottom.

Benefits of deep cleaning your house/Vehicle:
1.	Reduces allergies 
-	By deep cleaning, this reduces the possibility of allergies and respiratory problems by eliminating dust and other allergens from the surfaces that we touch, without cleaning these spots properly. This could trigger allergies or worsen respiratory conditions.

2.	Prevents germs from spreading 
-	Home cleaning plays a key role in eliminating bacteria and viruses from frequently touched surfaces like switches, doorknobs and countertops. Without thoroughly cleaning these areas, this increases a higher chance of bacteria spreading more rapidly.

3.	Improves air quality
-	By cleaning out your space this avoids buildup, meaning less particles will be found in the air. This is especially in surfaces such as carpets and curtains as they store allergens. Improved air quality in personal space means that they will be less allergic reactions and flu.

4.	Reduces stress and anxiety
-	When your personal space is cleaned, the happier you feel living in your space. A study showcases that a cluttered and dirty space makes it more difficult to focus on a task, mainly because you feel more overwhelmed so by cleaning out your space, this reduces stress and anxiety which increase productivity

Mission: 
Our Mission is to provide clients with thorough, environmentally friendly, and professional deep cleaning services. The attention to detail, honour, and satisfaction of our customers are the key to our success.

Vision:
Our vision is to transform personal spots into spotless, hygienic environments by setting the standard for innovation, quality, and reliability in the cleaning industry.

Target Audience:
Homeowners: This is the most common target audience for cleaning companies. Especially the renters. Perfect for moving in and moving out and cleaning out the tough spots that couldn’t be reached through normal cleaning
Vehicle detailing: This audience is common for car lovers who want to restore their car to have that new car feeling and look.
